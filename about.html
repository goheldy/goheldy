How to use NEEM WOOD COMB

1. Ensure that the Teeth Touch Your Scalp while Combing
2. Deep Combing Twice is Healthy
3. Do Not Expose Comb to Water
4. Keep Away from Direct Heat and Direct Sunlight
5. Always Wipe with Tissue or Cloth
6. It is Advisable to Change your Neem Wood Comb every 9 to 12 Months
