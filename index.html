WHY SHOULD WE USE NEEM WOOD COMB ?
While hair has a negative electrical charge, plastic combs and brushes have a positive charge, which draws hair to the comb and causes static or frizz.
The hair strands become dry and brittle from static and frizz, and they also appear untidy. In addition, unlike wood, plastic is hazardous for the environment. Neem wood combs prevent static harm to your hair since they are electrically inactive.

Combs made of NEEM WOOD contain carbon, much like your skin, hair, and scalp do. Additionally, because neem wood is organic, it is less likely to exacerbate skin issues or allergies that can be made worse by the use of plastic and metal combs.

The neem comb's massaging motion encourages the follicles to produce more hair strands, which promotes hair growth. A wooden comb doesn't develop tangles toward the end of the stroke like a plastic comb does.
